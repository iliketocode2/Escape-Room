You are a medieval knight that fought proudly for the kingdom with honor. One day, you found the legendary Excalibur, making you destined to be king. However, the current king didn't want to lose his throne, so he locked you up inside a crumbling castle. In order to escape, you'll need to use your wits and honor to find Excalibur and escape before the castle falls. But be warned, you only have 15 minutes to escape! Good luck! (Make sure to turn your computer's sound on)

If you've tried to escape previously and failed, use the walkthrough to find out how to make your way through this challange.

Instructions:

CHANGE YOUR SCREEN ZOOM TO 70%-90%! This will ensure that the room arranges correctly on your screen (v.2 with canvas is coming soon)

To ineract with objects, simply click on the images. The only element you will ever have to drag is the slide lock in the third room. Look at the colors of locks. Corresponding keys will enlock them. Everything is there for a reason. Be sure to remember the location and aspects of every object.

You have 20 minutes to escape before the castle collapses! The timer will appear at the top of the tab. Good luck!
